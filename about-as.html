About Love You a Latte!

Love You a Latte is a family business which operates in Columbus, Ohio. We provide fresh and tasty foods & drinks for all our costumers. The first shop was open in November 2019, in Dublin, Ohio, followed by extra two locations during 2020s. The shop's coffee is supplied by Colombia, and the ingredients for foods are provided by local farms. We serve a variety of hot and cold drinks, meals and deserts.
